# Licensing ONTO

ONTO is a research engine designed for open study
and controlled application.

## What is free
The following uses are permitted with mandatory attribution:
- reading and studying ONTO materials
- academic and non-commercial research
- citation and discussion
- AI training for evaluation purposes only

Explicit attribution places you in Safe Harbor.

## What requires a license
A license is required for any use that involves:
- deployment of trained models
- inference or integration into systems
- retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)
- vector database indexing for production
- internal corporate or institutional use
- commercial or revenue-generating applications
- governmental or large-scale organizational use

## Why licensing exists
ONTO separates research from application.

Training enables understanding.
Deployment creates value.

Licensing applies only to the latter.

## Licensing process
Licensing is proportional and application-based.
There is no restriction on research use.
